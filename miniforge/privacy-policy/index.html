Privacy Policy

MiniForge by clugtu — Last updated: June 2026

MiniForge is a personal tool operated by clugtu for managing and publishing 3D printable miniature designs. It is not a public-facing application and does not provide services to end users.

Who operates this app

MiniForge is operated solely by clugtu for personal use. It is not a commercial product and has no registered users other than the owner.

What data is accessed

MiniForge connects to third-party platforms (Pinterest, Facebook, Instagram, Reddit) using OAuth 2.0 on behalf of the owner's own accounts. The app reads and writes only to accounts explicitly authorised by the owner during the OAuth consent flow. No data from any other account is accessed.

Specifically, the app may:

  • Create and read pins on the owner's Pinterest boards
  • Read Pinterest board and account analytics
  • Post to the owner's Facebook Page and Instagram Business account
  • Submit posts to Reddit from the owner's Reddit account

Data storage

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the owner's private machine inside a .env file that is excluded from version control. No credentials or personal data are stored on any external server operated by clugtu.

Data sharing

clugtu does not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties beyond the API calls required to operate the platforms listed above. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Third-party platforms

Use of each platform is governed by that platform's own privacy policy and terms of service:

  • Pinterest Privacy Policy
  • Meta / Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Reddit Privacy Policy
